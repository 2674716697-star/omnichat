# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Chinese-speaking readers and writers using AI for interactive story creation and chat. Mobile-first, PWA, on-the-go usage.

## Product Purpose

Mira is an AI story chat client supporting multi-model chat, world-building character cards, branching story directions, and continuous memory. Users chat with AI models to co-create interactive narratives.

## Brand Personality

Refined, calm, focused. Dark glass-morphism aesthetic with a warm graphite-champagne palette. Champagne gold accent with subtle spring-based micro-interactions. Professional but warm — like a quiet writing studio at night with candlelight.

## Anti-references

- No bright/neon color schemes
- No cartoonish or gamified UI
- No cluttered SaaS dashboards with metrics everywhere
- No heavy marketing-style landing pages

## Design Principles

1. **Content-first**: the story is the focus; UI recedes
2. **Quiet confidence**: subtle material cues, not loud branding
3. **Mobile-native**: touch-first interactions, safe areas, keyboard-aware
4. **Smooth flow**: streaming text should feel alive, not jerky
5. **Depth without weight**: glass layers suggest space without heaviness

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA for text contrast
- Reduced motion support (prefers-reduced-motion)
- Chinese typography optimized (PingFang SC, Microsoft YaHei)
- Touch targets ≥ 44px on mobile
